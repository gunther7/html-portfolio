The Best Movies According to Richard

My top 3 movies of all-time

Young Frankenstein

This is my favorite movie of all. Classic actors and funny as hell lines.

Jaws

I saw this for the first time in a drive-in theatre in Indiana and it was amazing on a huge screen.

The Hateful Eight

Quentin Tarantino's take on a western is visually spectacular, the score is out of this world and the story line is unforgettable.
